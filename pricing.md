## Typical project ranges

| Service | Typical range (USD) | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| Custom kitchen cabinets (small kitchen) | $5,000 – $10,000 | Galley or small U-shape, basic materials |
| Custom kitchen cabinets (standard) | $10,000 – $20,000 | Mid-size kitchen, mid-range materials and finishes |
| Custom kitchen cabinets (premium) | $20,000 – $30,000+ | Large kitchen, premium hardwood, custom details, island |
| Kitchen full remodel | $15,000 – $40,000+ | Cabinets + coordination with countertops, lighting, layout |
| Bathroom cabinets / vanity | $1,500 – $6,000 | Single vanity, primary bathroom vanity, or linen tower |
| Closet & laundry room cabinets | $2,500 – $8,000 | Walk-in closet, reach-in system, or laundry built-ins |

Final price depends on cabinet count, materials (plywood vs. solid wood), door style, hardware, finish, and any structural modifications.

## What's included in every quote

- Cabinet boxes, doors, drawers, and drawer boxes
- Soft-close hinges and soft-close drawer slides on all doors/drawers
- Premium hardware (standard options; upgrades available)
- Design-to-install project management
- Delivery to the home
- Full installation by MJC's team
- Post-install walkthrough and punch-list completion
- Limited lifetime warranty on construction defects

## Not typically included (priced separately)

- Countertops (stone, quartz, solid surface) — MJC coordinates with preferred partners
- Electrical or plumbing modifications
- Appliance purchases
- Flooring, tile, or backsplash
- Demolition of existing cabinets (can be added)

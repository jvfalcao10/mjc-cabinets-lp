Our Story

How it works

From first call to perfect kitchen, without the headaches.

Most Orlando homeowners have been burned before: delays, wrong finishes, contractors who disappear. We built our process to fix exactly that.

01

Free in-home consultation

Our designer visits your home (or brings our mobile showroom van to you). We measure, we listen, we understand how you live. No cost, no commitment.

02

Photorealistic 3D design

Within days, you'll see a full 3D render of your new space — your measurements, your finishes, your layout. You approve every detail before we build a single piece.

03

Built in our Orlando factory

Our CNC machines cut your cabinets to millimeter precision. Every box is assembled, finished, and quality-checked in-house. No overseas shipping, no third-party suppliers.

04

Professional installation

Our own install crew (never subcontractors) delivers and installs. Clean, on time, and on budget. You walk into exactly the kitchen you approved in 3D.

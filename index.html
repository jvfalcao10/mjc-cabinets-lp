Questions, answered

Everything you need
to know, before you call.

How much do custom kitchen cabinets cost in Orlando?
Every kitchen is different, but most of our custom projects fall between $10,000 and $30,000+, depending on size, materials, and finishes. We offer 0% financing up to 15 months, so you can spread the investment without interest. The only way to get an accurate number is a free consultation — we'll measure, design in 3D, and give you a fixed quote.
How long does the full process take?
Typical timeline from first consultation to final install is 4–8 weeks: about 1–2 weeks for measurement and 3D design approval, 2–4 weeks for fabrication, and 1–2 days for installation. We lock the timeline before we start — no "we'll let you know" vibes.
Do you handle the installation, or do I need a contractor?
We handle everything — measurement, design, fabrication, delivery, and installation. You don't need a separate contractor for the cabinet work. If your project needs plumbing or electrical changes, we coordinate with trusted partners.
What areas do you serve around Orlando?
We serve the Greater Orlando area: Orlando, Kissimmee, Winter Park, Lake Mary, Altamonte Springs, Apopka, Oviedo, Clermont, and Sanford. If you're nearby and not sure, just call — we travel for the right project.
Do I need to move out during installation?
No. Most installations take 1–2 days and are done in your home without major disruption. We clean up every day and leave your space livable overnight. For larger full remodels, we'll discuss any temporary setup during the consultation.
Is there a warranty?
Yes. Our cabinets come with a limited lifetime warranty on construction defects and a separate warranty on hardware and finishes. We'll walk through everything in writing before installation so you know exactly what's covered.
What's included in the free consultation?
On-site measurement of your space, a discussion of your style and functional needs, material and finish options, and a free 3D design of the proposed layout. No obligation to move forward — you get the design to keep.
Can I keep my current appliances?
Absolutely. We design around existing appliances or new ones — whichever you prefer. If you're upgrading, we'll make sure the cabinet layout fits the new specs perfectly.
